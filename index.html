05

Terms explained

Plain-language meaning of words used on this rate card.

Add-onSomething extra added to your order for extra money — like adding suya to a plate of rice.
Lip-syncMaking the mouth in the video match the spoken words, so it looks like the person is really talking.
RevisionA change you ask for after seeing the first draft — like asking a tailor to shorten a sleeve.
Multi-shot sequenceThe video is made of several scenes joined together, instead of one continuous shot.
Fast turnaroundHow quickly the video is delivered — "fast" means same-day or next-day, not many days later.
